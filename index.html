News

  • Sept. 2016 - Glycan Therapeutics has added 32 new compounds to its catalog. Each new compound has an Azido functional group that can be used to couple with a flourescent or affinty tag. These new compounds are only available through Glycan Therapeutics.

  • Oct. 2015 - Glycan Therapeutics has been awarded a SBIR phase II contract from National Institute of Cancer with a total budget of close to $1 million over two years. The contract has commissioned the company to synthesize a library of heparan sulfate oligosaccharides for cancer-related research.
