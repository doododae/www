Custom Synthesis

We are the leader in the synthesis of heparin sulfate oligosaccharides and oligosaccharide derivatives. We offer the highest quality products at an affordable price including: Custom synthesis of homogeneous heparin and heparin sulfate oligosaccharides with a wide range of sizes and sulfation patterns for your research needs.

  • Synthesis of oligosaccharides outside of the current catalog
  • Special large quantity synthesis, up to 10 g scale synthesis of pure oligosaccharides
  • Stable isotopically labeled oligosaccharides with 13C-, 2H- or both 13C- and 2H-labeled oligosaccharides as reference standards for Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) and mass spectrometry analysis. Customer can choose the saccharide residue that carries the 13C-label and 2H-label.
  • Introduction of fluorescent labels at the reducing end or nonreducing end of the oligosaccharides.
  • Introduction of affinity tag, like biotin, to the reducing end or nonreducing end of oligosaccharides.
